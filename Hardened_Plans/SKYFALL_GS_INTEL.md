# SKYFALL GS INTELLIGENCE: Neural Scene Completion Volume
 
**Strategic Goal:** This volume documents the fusion of Gaussian Splatting and Diffusion (Flux) to reconstruct 3D environments in aerial-denied or unmapped regions. By utilizing neural scene completion, we ensure **Omni** maintains a complete digital twin of any target city.
 
---
 
## 🏗️ SATELLITE-TO-FACADE RECONSTRUCTION
 
### 1. The Skyfall Methodology
Skyfall GS utilizes a "Satellite-to-Facade" neural pipeline. It takes top-down satellite imagery and uses a trained Diffusion model to predict and "autocomplete" the ground-level facades of buildings. These autocompleted textures are then projected onto a 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) mesh.
 
### 2. Neural Mirrors & City Autocomplete
For cities with restricted ground access, Skyfall GS generates a "Neural Mirror"—a high-fidelity 3D representation that is architecturally consistent with the region's known styles (e.g., local soil color, infrastructure design). This ensures the **Omni Global Panopticon** has no "holes" in its 3D mesh.
 
### 3. Spatiotemporal Asset Projection
Once the mesh is generated, real-time records (GPS, Signal Telemetry) are projected onto the neural facade. This provides **Omni Insiders** with a high-fidelity "Street View" experience even in areas without explicit record coverage.
