## 🏗️ PRECISION VISUAL POSITIONING
 
### 1. Centimeter-Level Resolution
VPS solves the "5-Meter GPS Drift" problem by performing pixel-to-model feature matching. This provides the difference between knowing an asset is on a street versus knowing exactly which step of a staircase it occupies. We utilize this for "Spatially-Locked Annotations" in the **Omni 3D View**.
 
### 2. Navigation in Urban Canyons
In dense urban cores where GPS signals reflect off building facades, VPS utilizes visual landmarks to calculate absolute position and rotation. This ensures that the **Omni Global Panopticon** maintains a high-fidelity situational mirror even in GPS-restricted zones.
 
### 3. Distributed Visual Ingestion
We utilize a distributed network of authorized visual sensors to maintain a "Current Map" of the operational theater. This enables real-time awareness of architectural changes and temporary service obstructions.
