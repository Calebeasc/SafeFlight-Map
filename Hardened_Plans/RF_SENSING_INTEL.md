## 🏗️ THE PHYSICS OF CSI SENSING
 
### 1. Multipath Encoding & 802.11bf
CSI diagnostic records encode the "Spatial Fingerprint" of everything between a transmitter and receiver. By monitoring signal attenuation and phase shifts, the system detects the presence and volume of physical objects. We utilize the emerging **802.11bf** standard to turn authorized WiFi nodes into motion and presence sensors.
 
### 2. 3D Human Reconstruction (DensePose)
Utilizing "DensePose from WiFi" logic, the system reconstructs full 3D human body skeletons from signal fluctuations. This allows **Omni Insiders** to maintain tactical awareness of interiors and occluded spaces by rendering RF skeletons directly within the 3D city mesh.
 
### 3. Biometric RF Signatures (Identity Harmonization)
Every human body has a unique RF signature determined by mass and movement gait. By utilizing the "WhoFi" system, we can match these unique reflections to specific records, ensuring persistent asset awareness even when traditional identifiers are absent.
