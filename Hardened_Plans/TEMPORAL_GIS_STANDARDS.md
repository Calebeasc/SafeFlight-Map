# TEMPORAL GIS STANDARDS: Event Reconstruction Volume
 
**Strategic Goal:** This volume documents the implementation of 4D temporal tracking and high-fidelity event reconstruction. By utilizing "Time-Cursor" logic and spatiotemporal breadcrumbs, we provide **Omni** and **Oracle** with absolute historical awareness.
 
---
 
## 🏗️ 1. TIME-CURSOR LOGIC [IPHONE COMPATIBLE]
 
### A. 4D Record Replay
The interface includes a high-performance timeline slider that allows administrators to "rewind" and "replay" spatiotemporal events. This system uses temporal indexing to reconstruct the state of the operational environment at any specific moment in history.
 
### B. Temporal Synchronization
Ensuring that disparate data streams (e.g., signal bursts, movement records, and status logs) are perfectly aligned on the temporal axis for accurate forensic analysis.
 
---
 
## 🏗️ 2. SPATIOTEMPORAL BREADCRUMBS [IPHONE COMPATIBLE]
 
### A. Persistence & Decay
Every asset record includes "Breadcrumb" metadata—a trail of historical coordinates that visualize the asset's trajectory over time. The system supports adjustable decay rates to optimize UI performance in high-density areas.
 
### B. Pattern of Life Detection
Automated analysis of historical breadcrumbs to identify "Anomaly Events" or deviations from established organizational movement patterns.
