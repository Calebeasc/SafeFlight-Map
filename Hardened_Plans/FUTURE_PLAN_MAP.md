## 🧩 [OMNI-CORE] BOUNDED CONTEXT ARCHITECTURE
*To prevent the operational model from collapsing into one monolithic structure, the following 9 Bounded Contexts must be strictly enforced within the OMNI-CORE stack:*

- [ ] **Identity and Access Context:** Owns users, roles, clearances, auth sessions, environment mode restrictions, and capability grants.
- [ ] **Source and Ingestion Context:** Owns source connectors, feed metadata, ingestion jobs, parser definitions, and provenance metadata.
- [ ] **Entity Graph Context:** Owns entities, aliases, observations, links, relationship types, and deduplication/merge policies.
- [ ] **Geospatial and Timeline Context:** Owns location fixes, geometries, routes, overlays, time windows, map layers, and playback state.
- [ ] **Alert and Triage Context:** Owns alerts, alert severity, alert grouping, assignment, triage decisions, and SLA timers.
- [ ] **Case Management Context:** Owns cases, templates, evidence bundles, notes, tasks, timelines, and case status.
- [ ] **Workflow and Action Context:** Owns action definitions, parameters, policies, approval requirements, execution records, and writeback hooks.
- [ ] **Update and Platform Continuity Context:** Owns versions, signed packages, staged updates, snapshots, offline verification, and tray operations.
- [ ] **Audit and Governance Context:** Owns immutable logs, user activity, decision records, approval chains, and tamper evidence.
