# PROTECTIVE AWARENESS INTEL



---

## UNIVERSAL ANALYSIS PROTOCOL: TACTICAL UPDATES

### Feature: Temporal Presence Analysis
Identifying persistent diagnostic signatures across time-gated intervals to ensure asset safety and environmental stability. This allows administrators to detect unauthorized persistent monitoring of organizational nodes.
- **What it does:** Detects unauthorized persistent monitoring.
- **Implementation Effort:** Medium (Tangible).
- **Toolset:** Omni (Oversight).
- **Action Category:** Security Hardening.
 
### Feature: Service-Location Record Mapping [IPHONE COMPATIBLE]
Utilizing public record APIs to harmonize diagnostic records and identify environmental anomalies. This ensures that organizational assets are mapped accurately against the broader infrastructure grid.
- **What it does:** Maps and verifies the location of organizational assets.
- **Implementation Effort:** Medium.
- **Toolset:** Omni.
- **Action Category:** Administrative Awareness.
