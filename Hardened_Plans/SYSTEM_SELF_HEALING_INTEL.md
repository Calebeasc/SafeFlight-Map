## 🎯 SELF-HEALING & REMEDIATION PROTOCOLS
 
### 1. AUTONOMOUS RESOURCE REMEDIATION (DIAGNOSTIC TIER)
- **Obstruction Detection:** Real-time monitoring of physical hardware interfaces (COM ports, SDR, GPS) for hangs or non-responsive states.
- **Automated Driver Restoration:** 
    - **Administrative Override:** Triggering high-authority UAC prompts to gain necessary permissions for system-level repairs.
    - **Force Reset Logic:** Utilizing PowerShell/WMI to programmatically disable and re-enable malfunctioning device drivers, clearing hardware-level locks.
    - **Transient Error Mitigation:** Ensuring that minor hardware glitches are resolved instantly without mission interruption.
 
### 2. ENVIRONMENT INJECTION & RECOVERY
- **Path Injection Framework:** Utilizing `sys.path` injection to dynamically repair frozen or corrupted execution environments.
- **Standalone Binary Fixes:** Automated compilation of successful repair scripts into standalone binaries to prevent recurrence of identified failure modes.
- **Continuous Learning:** Developing a system that progressively adapts its remediation logic based on historical failure data.
