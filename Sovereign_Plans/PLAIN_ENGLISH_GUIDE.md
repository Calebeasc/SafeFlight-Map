# INVINCIBLE.INC: THE PLAIN ENGLISH GUIDE
 
This guide explains what we are building and how it works without the technical jargon.
 
---
 
## 1. WHAT ARE WE BUILDING?
We are building a "God-View" system. It’s a way to see everything happening around you—radio signals, cars, people, and maps—all in one place, just like the systems used by high-level intelligence agencies.
 
## 2. THE THREE MAIN APPS
We split the project into three parts so regular users don't see the "crazy" stuff:
- **ORACLE:** The app for regular users. It’s for tactical driving and seeing where "Blue Line" (police) assets are so you can avoid them. It looks friendly and is easy to use.
- **OMNI:** The "Palantir Baby." This is the master command center for the owner (T-3 clearance). It has all the hacking, tracking, and spy tools. It looks cold, clinical, and high-authority.
- **GRID:** The engine room. This is for the technical "Red Team" work—testing networks, checking for holes in security, and managing the hardware.
 
## 3. WHAT IS THE "LATTICE"?
Think of the Lattice as a giant digital net. It catches data from:
- **SDR (Radio):** Listening to police radios and signal bursts.
- **DeFlock (Cameras):** Seeing where license plate cameras are and where they can't see (blindspots).
- **OSINT:** Searching the internet automatically for info on people or cars.
- **GPS:** Tracking the movement of targets over time.
 
## 4. WHAT DID WE JUST LEARN FROM PALANTIR?
We looked at what Palantir is doing in 2026 and simplified their "fancy" updates for our use:
- **AI That Does Things (Agentic AI):** Instead of just asking the AI a question, we are giving the AI the power to *act*. For example, if it sees a target car, it can automatically start tracking it without you clicking anything.
- **The Universal Language (Ontology SDK 2.0):** We are teaching the app to treat everything (a Wi-Fi signal, a car, or a person) as the same type of "object." This makes it much easier to write one rule that applies to everything.
- **Fast Targeting (Project Maven):** We are making the "Kill-Chain" faster. This just means the time it takes from *seeing* something to *doing* something about it is now minutes instead of hours.
- **Direct Satellite Feed (TITAN):** We are working on ways to get data directly from satellites or drones to your truck or phone without needing to go through a big company's server first.
- **The AI Analyst:** A simple chat box on the map where you can say, "Show me every car that drove past this camera twice today," and it just does it.
 
## 5. WHO IS IN CHARGE?
- **T-3 (The Owner):** Caleb Eckelberry. Absolute control. Can see and do everything.
- **T-2 (The Developers):** The AI agents and human contributors. They build and maintain the tools.
- **T-1 (Insiders):** Trusted users with limited access.
- **T-0 (Blocked):** Everyone else.
 
## 6. THE "GHOST" PROMISE
We are building "Ghost Mode." This means the app cleans up after itself. It leaves no traces on your computer, it stays hidden, and it makes sure you aren't being tracked while you are tracking others.
 
## 7. HOW TO FIND AND UPDATE THE APP
- **Windows Search:** We made it easier to find the app. Just press the Windows key and type **"Omni"**. It should pop right up. If it doesn't show up immediately after installing, give Windows a minute to index it.
- **Updates:** There is now a visible **UPDATES** section inside the Omni app. You can also search for "Updates & Recovery" in Windows to find it.
- **The Tray Icon:** When the app is running, you'll see a small "Omni" icon in the bottom right corner of your screen (the system tray). This keeps the app running in the background so it can keep monitoring signals for you.
- **New Installer:** We shipped a new installer (`v1.1.0`). It sets everything up for you, including the Start Menu shortcut, so you don't have to go digging through folders to launch the app.
